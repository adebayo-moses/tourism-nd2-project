Vision 2025

Growing the Department's Vision

The Toursim Department is responsible for tourism information in LAGOS State Polytechnic.

It promotes the Institution as a domestic and international tourist destination for leisure, business, religion, festivals and commerce. To facilitate the growth of grassroots tourism, the corporation adopted a decentralized system of administration through the creation of zonal offices at Bauchi, Calabar, Kano, Lagos, Lokoja and Jos, each headed by a Zonal Coordinator.

Roadmap

Tourism is the fastest route to development and economic growth in the 21st century. In seeking to achieve a sustainable national tourism agenda, this roadmap lays the foundation that will open up Nigeria as a major tourism destination in Africa through the development of international tourism and promoting of domestic tourism. Students will be encouraged to spend their holidays in LAGOS and to know more about the Institution.

THE “CHIEF” PLAN

This is a 5-point action plan that aims to promote domestic tourism in the Institution.
Corporate Governance & Regulations
Introduce global best practice for the tourism industry through reviewing laws, education and standards.
Human Capital Development
Encourage the creation of jobs and engagement of Tourism stakeholders.
Infrastructural Development
Create, grow and sustain tourism assets.
Events & Marketing
Protect and promote Nigerians cultural heritage as a resource for home grown socio-economic development
Finance & Investment
Pursue funding for capital projects in the tourism sector.

The Leisure and Tourism brand is an ambitious, timely and visionary brand for promoting domestic tourism in LASPOTECH. The vision of the master brand is to build the premier online destination for authentic LASPOTECH content, using technology, creativity, arts and culture to push the new national agenda. It will showcase the true spirit of LASPOTECH and tell the visual story of the most populous black nation in the institution.
