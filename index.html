The Leisure and Tourism brand is an ambitious, timely and visionary brand for promoting domestic tourism in LASPOTECH. The vision of the master brand is to build the premier online destination for authentic LASPOTECH content, using technology, creativity, arts and culture to push the new national agenda. It will showcase the true spirit of LASPOTECH and tell the visual story of the most populous black nation in the institution.
